Catch Ten

Monthly Fishing Competition

The fishing competition for September is about catching ten fish of any species.

To participate in this event members must send nominations through to info@cardwellsportfishingclub.com or text to 0429 427 590 to enable registration for the event.

All participants will need to download and fill in a registration form together with the rules (see below) and then send the completed form and registration fee of $20 to info@cardwellsportfishingclub.com.
